About TechVision

TechVision is the annual science and technology festival of the Indian Institute of Technology, bringing together the brightest minds from around the world to celebrate innovation and technology.

With over 200 events spanning competitions, exhibitions, lectures, and workshops, TechVision provides a platform for students, professionals, and enthusiasts to showcase their talents and learn from the best in the industry.
